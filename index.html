Frequently Asked Questions

Is DVBible Game really free?
Yes. The game is completely free to play with absolutely no worldly ads. Support is voluntary for those who believe in the mission and want to help it grow.
Where does my donation go?
One hundred percent goes into game development, new upcoming Christian apps, server infrastructure, and ensuring the platform remains totally ad free for all players.
Can I support anonymously?
Absolutely. No need to share your name unless you want recognition.
How can international supporters be part of this mission?
Use the "Support from Outside Nigeria" button above to view contact instructions for our international ministry partners.
Does my support only go toward games?
No. We have over a hundred applications yet to be launched due to limited funding. Your support goes a long way in helping us launch and develop more utility driven applications designed to solve complex organizational challenges and create impactful educational tools. It also helps sustain our mission to reach nations for the Lord and expand platforms that inspire spiritual growth and transformation.
How else does my support make an impact?
Your support enables continuous development, testing, and improvement of our applications. It also helps us maintain quality, expand our reach, and ensure that our platforms remain accessible, impactful, and spiritually enriching.
Can I be part of the mission beyond giving?
Yes. You can support us through prayers, sharing our platforms with others, and engaging with our content. Every form of support contributes to advancing the mission and reaching more lives with meaningful, transformative tools.
